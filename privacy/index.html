✦ Privacy Policy ✦
Effective date: 1 June 2026  ·  Version 1.0

Mystica · 天机阁 is a fortune-telling and divination app for entertainment purposes. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and your rights. We are committed to keeping your information private and secure.

📋
1. Information We Collect

When you use Mystica, you may enter the following personal details to receive a personalised reading:

Data Why It's Needed Where It's Stored Shared?
Your name Personalises your reading Your device only Never
Date of birth Calculates zodiac signs & age Your device only Never
Gender Personalises reading language Your device only Never
Blood type (optional) Blood type reading only Your device only Never
Reading prompts Generate your AI reading Sent to AI (no name/DOB) Not retained

All personal details (name, date of birth, gender, blood type) are stored exclusively on your device using browser localStorage. They are never transmitted to our servers.

🔮
2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to generate your personalised fortune reading within the app. Specifically:

• Your name, date of birth, and gender remain on your device and are used to construct a reading prompt locally.

• That reading prompt (which describes your zodiac sign and reading type, but does not include your name or date of birth) is sent to our secure backend server, which forwards it to the Anthropic Claude AI to generate your reading.

• We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than delivering your reading.

🤖
3. AI-Generated Readings Disclaimer

All readings in Mystica are generated by artificial intelligence (Anthropic Claude). They are provided for entertainment purposes only and should not be used as a substitute for professional advice — including medical, legal, financial, or psychological guidance.

Mystica makes no guarantee of accuracy. The readings are creative, AI-generated content and do not predict the future.

🔒
4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties for marketing purposes.

The only third-party service involved in operating Mystica is Anthropic (the AI provider). Our backend sends reading prompts to Anthropic's API. These prompts do not contain your name, date of birth, or any other directly identifying information. Anthropic's own privacy policy applies to any data processed by their API.

Our backend server is hosted on Render.com. No personal data is logged or stored by our server.

🗓️
5. Data Retention & Deletion

Since all personal data is stored on your device (via localStorage), you are in full control. You can delete your data at any time by:

• Clearing the app's data in your device Settings → Apps → Mystica → Clear Data, or

• Uninstalling the app.

We do not retain any personal data on our servers. Server logs (IP address, timestamp) used for rate limiting are held in memory only and are automatically cleared daily.

👶
6. Children's Privacy

Mystica is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can assist.

🛡️
7. Security

All communication between the app and our backend server uses HTTPS encryption. Since personal data is stored locally on your device and not on our servers, the risk of a server-side data breach affecting your personal information is minimal.

⚖️
8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, or delete it. Since all data is stored on your own device, you can exercise these rights directly without contacting us.

If you have questions about data we may hold (such as server logs), please contact us using the details below.

📝
9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
